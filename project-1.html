kathmandu

Kathmandu is renowned for its historical and cultural heritage. The Kathmandu Valley, a UNESCO World Heritage site, includes seven monument zones: the Durbar Squares of Kathmandu, Patan, and Bhaktapur; the Buddhist stupas of Swayambhu and Bauddhanath; and the Hindu temples of Pashupati and Changu Narayan. These sites are famous for their unique architectural styles, intricate carvings, and religious significance, representing a blend of Hinduism and Buddhism that has evolved over centuries​ (Encyclopedia Britannica)​​ (UNESCO World Heritage Centre

Pokhara

Pokhara, located in central Nepal, is a picturesque city known for its natural beauty and vibrant culture. It is the second most populous city in Nepal, with around 599,504 residents, and serves as the capital of Gandaki Province​ (Wikipedia)​. Situated approximately 200 kilometers west of Kathmandu, Pokhara is a popular tourist destination and the starting point for many treks in the Annapurna region​ (Wikipedia)​​ (Wikivoyage
